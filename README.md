## General Resources

* **[The Marketing Newsletter](https://themarketingnewsletter.org)** – Weekly strategies on marketing-tech, attribution, and growth.
* **[What is GTM Engineering?](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/gtm-engineering)** – A breakdown of this emerging role.
* **[Growth Engineering Handbook](https://amplitude.com/blog/growth-engineering)** – Building systems for growth and analytics.

## GTM Engineering Foundations

* **[Segment Docs](https://segment.com/docs/)** – Customer data infrastructure basics.
* **[RudderStack Docs](https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/)** – Open-source Segment alternative.
* **[dbt for Marketing](https://www.getdbt.com/blog/dbt-marketing-data-models/)** – Data transformation for marketing teams.

## Attribution & Tracking

* **[Snowplow](https://snowplow.io/)** – First-party event tracking infrastructure.
* **[UTM.io](https://utm.io/)** – Manage and track UTM parameters at scale.
* **[Funnel](https://funnel.io/)** – Multi-touch attribution and marketing data unification.
* **[Open Pixel](https://openpixel.dev/)** – Open-source pixel tracking library.

## Analytics & Event Instrumentation

* **[PostHog](https://posthog.com/)** – Product analytics + session recording with full API.
* **[Amplitude](https://amplitude.com/)** – Event tracking and growth analytics.
* **[Heap](https://heap.io/)** – Autocapturing event data with retroactive analysis.
* **[Plausible](https://plausible.io/)** – Lightweight, privacy-first web analytics.

## Web Tagging & GTM Platforms

* **[Google Tag Manager](https://tagmanager.google.com/)** – Widely used platform for deploying marketing tags.
* **[Segment](https://segment.com/)** – Data routing and tagging across tools.
* **[TagCommander](https://www.commandersact.com/)** – Enterprise-level tag and consent management.
* **[Tealium iQ](https://tealium.com/products/tealium-iq-tag-management/)** – Tag management system with enterprise integrations.

## Marketing Automation APIs

* **[HubSpot API](https://developers.hubspot.com/docs/api/overview)** – Automate contacts, forms, emails, and tracking.
* **[Marketo REST API](https://developers.marketo.com/rest-api/)** – Sync data across lead lifecycle and attribution.
* **[Customer.io](https://customer.io/docs/api/)** – Trigger messages via API.
* **[SendGrid](https://docs.sendgrid.com/)** – Email campaign and transactional email automation.

## Growth Engineering Tools

* **[Clearbit](https://clearbit.com/)** – Enrichment APIs for leads, traffic, and personalization.
* **[Mutiny](https://www.mutinyhq.com/)** – Website personalization with no-code rules and APIs.
* **[VWO](https://vwo.com/)** – Testing and experimentation platform.
* **[Statsig](https://www.statsig.com/)** – Feature gating + experimentation.

## Data Infrastructure

* **[dbt](https://www.getdbt.com/)** – Transform raw data for GTM analytics.
* **[Airbyte](https://airbyte.io/)** – Open-source ELT for syncing GTM tools.
* **[Fivetran](https://fivetran.com/)** – Data pipelines from SaaS tools to your warehouse.
* **[Metabase](https://www.metabase.com/)** – Visual dashboarding for marketing/product data.

## Experimentation & A/B Testing

* **[Optimizely](https://www.optimizely.com/)** – Robust platform for testing, targeting, and personalization.
* **[GrowthBook](https://www.growthbook.io/)** – Open-source feature flags and A/B testing.
* **[Trial-to-Paid Conversion Sample Size Calculator](https://trial-to-paid-conversion-sample-size-calculator.vercel.app/)** – No-signup calculator for planning SaaS trial-to-paid A/B test sample size, expected paid conversions, and readout timing.
* **[Split.io](https://www.split.io/)** – Controlled rollouts and experimentation.
* **[Google Optimize (Sunset)](https://optimize.google.com/)** – Free testing platform (deprecated in 2023).
